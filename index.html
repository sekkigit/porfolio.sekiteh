About me

I like programming. And the best way I've found to have a positive impact on code is to write it.

For the last eleven years I have been working at the Ina Oil Refinery as an operator, and as a process manager at the Energy branch.
In addition to work and family, I work hard to expand my programing knowledge, and for the past three years, I’ve been focused on learning about cloud solutions like AWS.
I enrolled in an online course for AWS Certified Solutions Architect - Associate at “A Cloud Guru” where I had the opportunity to learn by doing.
I see myself in this profession because the topics I studied are very interesting and I enjoyed learning and researching.
In addition to education, I have fun with my projects on my own HomeLab server and I learned various new tools such as Docker, Terraform, Nginx, Git, Bash and many others.

My goal is to find a job that will suit my desires and abilities and provide professional education.
